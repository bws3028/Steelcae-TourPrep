TOUR PREPARATION & STRATEGY

(Reference questions)

BACKGROUND

  • Who is the client?
  • What type of group is coming in?
  • Who will be the decision makers?
  • What is the scope, timing and competition for the project?

NEEDS & ISSUES/OBSTACLES

  • What issues has the client been running into lately?
  • Are there specific aspects of the classroom they want to focus on?

EXPLORE/CONFIRMATION

  • Other than the client's mentioned interests, ask about exploring different things.
  • "Have you thought about this before?"
  • "You mentioned that you wanted ___, is that correct?"

ADDITIONAL/ETC.

  • What would put Steelcase above the competitor(s)?
  • If their budget was not a constraint, what else would the client like to see completed?
  • Has the client tried other solutions before?
